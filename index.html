Origin and Growth

  • All this is started with me, a mother of two Kids who suffered with Mental Retardation due to Jevenile Epilepsy(fits). These kids never going to have a normal life. They would never eat, walk or talk like regular children. But deep in my heart, I knew that they were the same innocent children as they could love, laugh and cry like all of us.
    When they were old enough to go to schoo in 1996, there was no place in Warangal District, ready to take them and give any kind of education or training . These children needed special attention and support so that they also attain mear normal life.

    That was when I started this effort, so that my children along with other similars have a home where they are endowed with abundant love and attention they rightfully deserve.
    After that, I struggled to obtain a diploma in Special Education for Mental Retardation, and started Mallikamba with the help and blessings from several people and Mrs.Padmaja Reddy, wife of Mr.K.Prabhakar Reddy, I.A.S has been a God Mother for us raising funds since we started. Her commitment and love for the children is ever increaing, there was big encouragement from my family members.
    Mallikamba Manovikasa Kendram is a home to an increasing group of Children with different kinds of special needs. We have a team of qualified trained staff and a equally committed team of helping staff. Together, they provide children at the institue love and support so that they an have a joyful life.
    Though, all the 220 children and the dedicated staff are in an abode that attempts to transform their lives, there are a few more necessities that are still to be provided.
    I humbly request generous people like you to come forward and support our cause. We are soliciting contributions to the corpus to make the institution self sustained, your valuable donation is highly appreciated and it will be exempted under section 80G of the Income Tax Act, 1961.
